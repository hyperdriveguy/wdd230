Tornado Damages Iconic Statue

In Preston, Idaho, the Napleon Dynamite statue is the city's biggest claim to fame. As the result of a recent passing tornado, the statue is severely damaged.

"I saw the head fly off, followed by each limb," said one eye witness.

"I could of sworn the cloud that tornado came from looked like a llama," said another witness, "I just don't think that's a co-inky-dink[sic]."
