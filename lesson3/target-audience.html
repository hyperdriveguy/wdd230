Yeet Weather Target Audience

Personas

Persona 1

Napoleon is a young adult male residing in Preston, Idaho. He has some college education. He has little knowledge on the subject of weather and weather forecasting. He needs to regularly check the weather forecast every few days to see the predicted weather to see if any storms are coming. Napoleon primarily will view the site on a standard desktop computer.

Persona 2

Aubrey is an older female visiting Fish Haven, Idaho. She has completed higher level education. She is somewhat knowledgable in weather and weather forecasts. She wants to check the current weather at a moment's notice to see if the day will be good for fishing or boating with her husband. She wants live statistics on current temperature, wind speed, wind direction, air pressure, and sky conditions.

Scenarios

  • What is the current temperature in Soda Springs, Idaho?
  • What will the weather be like on Friday in Preston, Idaho?
  • Is it windy near Fish Haven, Idaho?
  • What did the storm last July look like?
  • What was the weather like 3 months ago in Preston, Idaho?
